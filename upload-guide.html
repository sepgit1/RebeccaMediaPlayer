💖 Rebecca's Song Upload Guide 💖

🎵 Current Status: Your Rebecca Media Player already has built-in song upload! No additional storage or website needed for basic use.

📱 Method 1: Direct Upload (Simplest)

Rebecca can upload songs directly in the app:

  1. Open Rebecca Media Player
  2. Click the "📁 Add Songs" button
  3. Select audio files from her device
  4. Songs are stored locally and ready to play!

Supported formats: MP3, WAV, AAC, OGG, and most audio files

💾 Local Storage (Current System)

The app currently uses:

  • Browser Storage: Songs stored locally in browser
  • Device Memory: Files stay on Rebecca's device
  • Offline Access: Works without internet
  • Privacy: No data sent to external servers

🔄 Transfer Methods (If Needed)

If Rebecca wants songs from another device:

  • USB Drive: Copy files to USB, then upload
  • Email: Send files to herself, download on device
  • Cloud Drive: Upload to Google Drive/Dropbox, download
  • AirDrop (iOS): Wireless transfer between Apple devices
  • Nearby Share (Android): Wireless transfer between Android devices
❓ Do You Need Additional Storage?
